My Chickens!


Game Description:


You play as small chick named Jay. You live in a small chicken town inhabited by other chicks and a Mother Hen. You normally live a very peaceful life where the Mother Hen provides an abundance of food for the whole town but it seems a small problem has arisen... Explore the town, help out the other chickens and uncover the mystery of what has happened to the town!

Controls:

  • Z: Select and interact with NPC's, Item's and Environment
  • X: Cancel interaction

  • Movement :
    • ↑ , ↓, ←, → Arrow Keys to move your character in arrow keys' respective direction.
    • W, A, S, D can also be used for movement in the same orientation as the arrow keys.
